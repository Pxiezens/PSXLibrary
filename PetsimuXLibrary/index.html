Welcome to the library
Pet Simulator X is a game made by BIG Games Simulators. A player starts the game with a Cat or Dog as a pet. After choosing their pet, players collect coins and chests throughout the map to buy new areas to venture or buy new eggs to get more pets for trading or collecting. The game then had two sequels: Pet Simulator 2 and Pet Simulator X.
